About Tea

Black Tea originated in China during the Ming dynasty. It became popular worldwide via British trade and is known for its rich, bold flavor and higher oxidation.

Green Tea dates back over 4,000 years in China and Japan. It's made from unoxidized leaves and is cherished for its delicate taste and health benefits.
